Hi there, I'm Daniel Groves I'm a developer and photographer based in southwest england who is addicted to the mountains

Developer

I develop clean and maintainable code for my own personal projects, open-source contributions, and my employer.

I work in a number of languages including C#, Python, Ruby and Java. I have experience with web frameworks such as Django, Ruby on Rails and ASP.net.

I use GIT for version control in all of my projects to ensure that it is easily to collaborate with other developers, experiment new with features and control changes.

Photographer

I am a keen photographer who spends as much time shooting and editing photographs as breathing.

Although primerily a landscape photosgrapher, I have also been known to dabble in street and action-sport photography.

In order to help push my photography to the next level I am working to complete a Photo365 challenge over 2015.

Writer

Despite spending much of my time writing code and taking photographs I frequently set time aside to read and write. After any significant adventure or photoshoot I tend to docuemnt them on my adventures and photography page.

I also regularly write about technical challenges and projects on which I have embarked in attempt to document things for my own future reference, as well as with the small chance that they will aid somebody else.
